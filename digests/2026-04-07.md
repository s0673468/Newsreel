## AI Technical Discourse

**Karpathy's "LLM Wiki" goes viral — 12M+ views, knowledge management > RAG**
Karpathy proposed replacing RAG with persistent LLM-maintained wikis: instead of retrieving from raw docs at query time, the LLM incrementally builds and maintains a structured markdown knowledge base. New sources get read, extracted, and integrated — updating entities, revising summaries, noting contradictions. His research wiki: ~100 articles, 400K words. The key insight: compile knowledge once, keep it current, don't re-derive on every query.
[VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/data/karpathy-shares-llm-knowledge-base-architecture-that-bypasses-rag-with-an) | [Analytics Vidhya](https://www.analyticsvidhya.com/blog/2026/04/llm-wiki-by-andrej-karpathy/)

> **Personal relevance:** This is essentially what Newsreel does — `covered_stories.txt` and `seen_urls.txt` are a primitive version of this pattern. Worth considering a structured knowledge graph layer on top.

## APOE4 & Neurodegeneration

**APOE4 causes early hippocampal hyperexcitability via Nell2 protein — new therapeutic target (Nature Aging)**
Young APOE4 knockin mice show hippocampal region-specific network hyperexcitability that predicts later cognitive deficits. The mechanism: APOE4 increases Nell2 protein production, causing neuronal hyperactivity and shrinkage. Critically, reducing Nell2 restores normal function, and selectively removing neuronal APOE4 eliminates the early phenotype entirely. Published April 3, 2026.
[Nature Aging](https://www.nature.com/articles/s43587-026-01096-0) | [Medical Xpress](https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-04-alzheimer-gene-disrupts-brain-circuits.html)

> **Personal relevance:** This is mechanistically important for APOE4/4 carriers. The Nell2 pathway is a new druggable target. The "hyperexcitability predicts later deficits" framing suggests early intervention could change trajectory. Worth tracking whether any Nell2 inhibitors enter preclinical pipelines.
