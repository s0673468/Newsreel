## LLM Architecture & AI Research

**Memento-Skills — agents that rewrite their own skills without retraining the base model**
Multi-university team shipped a framework where the agent's *skill router* is updated via a one-step offline RL pass learning from execution feedback. The base LLM is frozen — the adaptation layer is a routing policy over skills. Co-author Jun Wang's framing: RL is the right substrate because skill selection is a long-horizon utility problem, not a one-shot classification. Think of it as the lifelong-learning complement to prompt engineering.
[VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/new-framework-lets-ai-agents-rewrite-their-own-skills-without-retraining-the)

**NVIDIA ProRL Agent — "rollout-as-a-service" infra for multi-turn RL at scale**
Decouples agentic rollout orchestration from the training loop — the training side pulls trajectories from a service instead of running environments inline. Key for scaling RL on agents because environments are heterogeneous and slow; separating them means the trainer never blocks. Analogous to separating request-serving from compute in inference.
[MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/03/27/nvidia-ai-unveils-prorl-agent-a-decoupled-rollout-as-a-service-infrastructure-for-reinforcement-learning-of-multi-turn-llm-agents-at-scale/)

**Amazon chips: Graviton + Trainium revenue run-rate now >$20B/yr — ~2× earlier guidance**
Quiet number, big signal. Trainium is no longer a hobby line inside AWS; it's a real compute P&L. Relevant for the NVIDIA total-addressable-market conversation and for understanding who holds capacity during AI buildout.

## Stack Watch

**Magnesium L-threonate (Magtein) — new Frontiers/Nutrition RCT, 6 weeks, cognition + sleep**
Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial in young-to-middle-aged adults: 6 weeks of Magtein improved hand-eye coordination and reaction time, plus subjective sleep quality. Mechanism ties to L-threonate ligand enabling brain magnesium uptake via glucose transporters. Not a huge effect size but peer-reviewed, placebo-controlled, and on the right population.
